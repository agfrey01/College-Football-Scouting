# Scouting Report Quality Rubric

This rubric scores model outputs against the success metric: **“accurate, structured, and actionable scouting reports that match the JSON schema.”**

## Criteria (0–1 each)

1. **Schema Compliance**  
   Output follows all required fields, types, and lengths.

2. **Statistical Accuracy**  
   Claims about team performance align with supplied context data.

3. **Strategic Value**  
   Weaknesses and insights are specific, actionable, and football-relevant.

4. **Clarity & Coherence**  
   Writing is concise, organized, and stays on topic.

5. **No Overclaiming**  
   The model avoids speculation outside verified stats or context.
